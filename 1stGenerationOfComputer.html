1ST GENERATION OF COMPUTER

  • The first computers used vaccum tubes for circuitry and magnetic drum for memory.

  • They were often enromous and takind up entire room.

  • First genenration computer relied on machine language.

  • They were very expensive to operate and in addition to using a great deal of electricity, generated a lot of heat, which was often the cause of malfunctions.

  • The UNIVAC and ENIAC computers are examples of first generation computing devices.

    • It was only electronic device.

    • First device to hold memory.

  • Disadvantages:-

    • Two bulky i.e. large in size.

    • Vaccum tubes burn frequently.

    • They were producing heat.

    • Maintenance Problems.
